📋 Panduan Bercerita Klik untuk artinya

Ikuti langkah ini saat bercerita (Follow these steps when telling your story)

  • 1. Perkenalkan dirimu 👋
    Introduce yourself (name, where you're from)
  • 2. Kamu pergi ke mana? 🗺️
    Where did you go?
  • 3. Kamu pergi dengan siapa? 👨‍👩‍👧
    Who did you go with?
  • 4. Naik apa ke sana? 🚗
    What transportation did you take?
  • 5. Kamu ngapain aja di sana? 🎡
    What did you do there?
  • 6. Berapa biayanya? 💰
    How much did it cost?
  • 7. Bagaimana perasaanmu? 😊
    How did you feel about the trip?
