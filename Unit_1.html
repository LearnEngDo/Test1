Boleh vs Bisa

Boleh (Permission) vs Bisa (Ability)
Bisa = ability — talks about a skill or what your body/mind can do.
Boleh = permission — talks about what is allowed by rules or another person.
Easy way to remember: Bisa = Can I (skill). Boleh = May I (permission).

Ada vs Tidak Ada

There Is vs There Isn't
Ada = there is / there are (something exists).
Tidak ada = there isn't / there aren't (nothing exists).
"Ada" can sometimes replace "punya" (to have). Example: "Aku ada makanan di rumah" = "I have food at home."
