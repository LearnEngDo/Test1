💡 Key tip: Banget

Add banget after almost any adjective to intensify it — like "really" or "so" in English. Seru banget! (So fun!), Capek banget (Really tired), Keren banget! (So cool!)

Discourse Markers

Jadi — 3 different uses!

(1) So / Therefore: Hujan, jadi kita batal pergi. (It rained, so we cancelled.)
(2) Did it happen? (confirmation): Kamu jadi beli HP baru? (Did you end up buying a phone?)
(3) Still happening? (checking plans): Kita jadi nonton? (Are we still watching?)

Ngomong-ngomong — "By the way..."

Use at the start of a sentence to change topic or bring up something new, just like "BTW" in English.
Ngomong-ngomong, gimana kabar orangtua kamu? = By the way, how are your parents?

Mungkin — "Maybe" AND "Possible"

Maybe: Mungkin dia lupa. = Maybe she forgot.
Possible: Apa itu mungkin? = Is that possible?
Impossible: Nggak mungkin! = Impossible! / No way!

Real Conversations

Read these dialogues and notice how slang and expressions fit naturally into everyday chat.

📱 Making Plans · Using "jadi" + excitement words
AEh, kamu jadi ke konser bulan depan nggak?Hey, are you still going to the concert next month?
BJadi dong! Aku udah nggak sabar banget. Kamu?Of course! I really can't wait. You?
AJadi! Tapi temenku kayaknya nggak jadi ikut. Kasihan banget.Yes! But my friend might not come anymore. Such a shame.
BMasa? Kenapa?Really? Why?
AGara-gara ada kerjaan mendadak. Duh, nggak banget.Because of sudden work. Ugh, that sucks.
BYauda, kita pergi berdua aja. Gas!Nevermind, let's just go the two of us. Let's go!
☕ Lazy Sunday · Mager expressions
ANgopi yuk! Ada cafe baru yang seru banget.Let's grab coffee! There's a new cafe that looks so fun.
BDuh, mager pol. Jauh nggak?Ugh, I'm so lazy. Is it far?
ALewat tol sekitar 20 menit aja.Via the toll road, around 20 minutes.
BMales ah. Lagi nggak mood. Pengen rebahan aja hari ini.Can't be bothered. Not in the mood. Just want to lie down today.
ADih, nggak asik! Ntar sore gimana?Ugh, no fun! What about this afternoon?
BNanti aja lah. Aku kabarin kamu.Maybe later. I'll let you know.
😲 Surprising News · Disbelief expressions
AEh, tau nggak? Aku dapet tiket gratis konser ke luar negeri!Hey, you know what? I got a free ticket to an overseas concert!
BSumpah?! Ke mana?No way?! Where to?
AKe Jepang! Aku iseng ikut lotere, eh menang.To Japan! I entered the lottery just for fun, and I won.
BSerius?! Gokil banget! Kapan?Seriously?! That's insane! When?
ABulan depan. Nggak sabar banget!Next month. I can't wait at all!
BKeren banget! Syukurlah! Kamu emang beruntung.So cool! Thank goodness! You're really lucky.
😔 Disappointment · Sad expressions
AMarshel tadi tiba-tiba batalin janji makan bareng. Padahal aku udah siap-siap.Marshel suddenly cancelled our lunch plan. I'd already gotten ready.
BKok gitu sih? Dia bilang kenapa?How could he do that? Did he say why?
AKatanya ada urusan penting. Tapi ternyata dia cuma ngopi sama temen.He said it was something important. But turns out he was just having coffee with friends.
BNggak banget! Kecewa banget pasti.That's awful! You must be so disappointed.
AIya. Ngga nyangka dia bisa gitu.Yeah. I didn't expect him to do that.
